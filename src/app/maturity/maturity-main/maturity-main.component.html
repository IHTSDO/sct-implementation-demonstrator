Welcome to SNOMED International’s Implementation Maturity Assessment Tool

The SNOMED International Implementation Maturity Assessment Tool is designed to help Vendors, Members (National Release Centers), and User Organizations evaluate their progress in adopting and integrating SNOMED CT. This tool provides structured insights into implementation maturity, highlighting strengths and areas for improvement.

Why use this tool?

Identify gaps, gain insights, align with best practices, and advance SNOMED CT adoption—tailored for vendors, members, and user organizations.

Why use this tool?
Identify Gaps – Assess implementation challenges and pinpoint areas needing further development.
Obtain actionable Insights – Receive customized recommendations to advance SNOMED CT adoption.
Align with Best Practices – Benchmark your progress against internationally recognized standards.
Tailored for Different Stakeholders – Designed to support key stakeholders in their SNOMED CT journey.
How does it work?

Assess key areas, receive a maturity score, gain improvement insights, and generate a report to enhance SNOMED CT implementation and collaboration.

How does it work?
Select Key Process Areas – Choose the areas most relevant to your role and implementation needs.
Complete the Assessment – Answer structured questions across selected key process areas.
Receive a Maturity Score – Get a clear view of your current implementation maturity level.
Access Improvement Recommendations – Receive targeted guidance to enhance your SNOMED CT deployment.
Download and Share Your Report – Generate a detailed report which can be stored for internal use or shared.
Who should use it?

Tailored assessments for vendors, members, and user organizations to evaluate SNOMED CT integration, adoption, and clinical use.

Who should use it?
Vendors – Assess SNOMED CT integration within healthcare software and applications.
Members (National Release Centers) – Evaluate national or regional SNOMED CT adoption and management strategies.
User Organizations – Analyze SNOMED CT use within healthcare systems, EHRs, and clinical workflows.
